About Sudhanshu Pani

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Business Management, NMIMS, Mumbai. My academic journey has been enriched by various institutions, including:

  • PhD from School of Business Management, Mumbai, NMIMS University (2016-2022)

  • ExePGP from Indian Institute of Management, Indore (2006-2008)

  • B.E. from Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology, Sarang, Utkal University (1995-1999)

My professional background is diverse, having worked with major financial institutions like ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, Standard Chartered Bank, and HDFC Standard Life. I've also ventured into entrepreneurship, bringing a practical perspective to my academic pursuits.

Research Interests:

  • Market microstructure

  • Consumer Finance

  • Capital Markets

  • Wealth Management

  • Corporate Finance

  • Systems Thinking

  • Sustainability

I am passionate about integrating theory with practical insights to contribute to both academia and industry. My work has led to the publication of several articles, book chapters, and case studies, focusing on innovative financial strategies and sustainable business practices.
